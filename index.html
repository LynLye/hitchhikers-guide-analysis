The Statistical Guide to Hitchhiker's

A statistical analysis of Douglas Adams' trilogy in five parts

Why?

I would just as soon ask "Why not?" The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy series has been a favorite of mine for many years. I happened to find the full text online and decided to see what kind of interesting data I could pull from it.
